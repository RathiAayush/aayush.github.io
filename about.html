About me

Hello. I am Gurshabad Grover. I work at the intersection of software design analysis and human rights at the Centre for Internet & Society.

I was a Legislative Assistant to Member of Parliament (LAMP) Fellow 2017-18. In my time at the office of Mr. V. Vijayasai Reddy, I prepared legislative briefs, researched about policy issues and wrote (mostly human rights) legislation that was introduced in the Parliament.

Before that, I studied computer science at IIIT-Delhi where my undergraduate thesis (advised by Prof. Rahul Purandare) was on developing program analysis tools to detect and remove superfluous information from robots' communication channels.
