About

Hello. I am a technolgist and legal researcher based in Bangalore, India. As an Information Controls Fellow, my current research (hosted at the Open Observatory of Network Interference), aims to uncover how internet service providers exacerbate the effects of state-ordered censorship.

Previously, I worked at CIS India, where my writing focused on censorship and surveillance in India, network security & privacy, and internet governance. Before that, I was a Legislative Assistant to Member of Parliament (LAMP) Fellow.
